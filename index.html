Heating Up?

Climate change is real! This map aims to help visualize how climate has changed over the decades.

Choose a starting decade and an ending decade, then hit the compare button to see the difference between the annual average temperature °F of those decades, per county.



  • Climate Data from NOAA
  • County and State boundaries from U.S. Census
